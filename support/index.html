Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start a quick workout?
    Go to the first screen, set time & rounds, and press start.
  • How can I create custom workouts?
    Go to the Workouts tab and tap the "+" button.
  • No sound?
    Check mute switch and volume. Also ensure app has audio permission.
